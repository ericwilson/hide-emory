Welcome to HIDE

HIDE (Health Information DE-identification) is a framework for de-identifying medical data. HIDE provides an annotation tool that can be used to manually or automatically de-identify medical records and manage trained models. HIDE is currently under heavy development and more features are coming soon. Click around and have fun.
